# Rate Limits

<Info>
  If you require higher limits, please contact us at [support@caesar.xyz](mailto:support@caesar.xyz).
</Info>

<Note>
  **Scope:** These limits apply **only** to <code>POST /research</code> (create research). Other endpoints are not rate-limited under this policy.
</Note>

The Caesar API has the following limits during the beta period:

- **Concurrent research jobs:** 5 per account (in-progress jobs, including `queued`).
- **Monthly compute budget:** 200 compute units, counted only for **successful** research jobs.

## Exceeding limits

When a limit is exceeded the API returns **429 Too Many Requests**:

```json
{ "error": "Too many requests — rate limit exceeded." }
````

**Recommended client behaviour**

* Retry with exponential back-off.
* Serialise new jobs when you’re at the concurrency cap.
* Use `compute_units` thoughtfully to stay within your monthly budget.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a compute unit?">
    A compute unit represents the effort allocated to a research job. Your monthly usage is the sum of the `compute_units` for jobs that reach the `completed` status.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What counts as ‘concurrent’?">
    Any job that is not yet `completed` or `failed` (e.g. `queued`, `searching`, `summarising`, `analysing`) counts towards your concurrency limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When do monthly limits reset?">
    Limits reset on a monthly cadence; your dashboard shows your remaining balance and the next reset window.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I request higher limits?">
    Yes. Email <a href="mailto:support@caesar.xyz">support@caesar.xyz</a> with your expected volumes and use case.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I see my remaining quota?">
    Visit <a href="https://ask.caesar.xyz/app/api-keys">ask.caesar.xyz/app/api-keys</a> after signing in.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
